What is the Book of Deuterronomy?
The Book of Deuteronomy is the fifth book of the Torah, where it is called Devarim (Hebrew: "the words [of Moses]".
and the fifth book of the Christian Old Testament, where it is also known as the Fifth Book of Moses.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Book_of_Deuteronomy|[ref 1]]]
