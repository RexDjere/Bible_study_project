What was Moab?
Moab is the name of an ancient kingdom whose territory is today located in the modern state of [[+Jordan]] .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moab|[ref 1]]]


What was the Moabite capital?
The Moabite capital was [[+Dibon]] .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moab|[ref 1]]]

According to the Hebrew Bible, who eas Moab often in conflict with?
According to the Hebrew Bible, Moab was often in conflict with its Israelite neighbours to the west.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moab|[ref 1]]]
