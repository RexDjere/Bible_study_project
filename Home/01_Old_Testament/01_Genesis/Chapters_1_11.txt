What does the first part of Genesis describe?
"The first part of Genesis describes humanity's downward spiral into sin,
but it also offers a future hope of restoration and redemption."	[[https://bibleproject.com/explore/video/genesis-1-11/|[ref 1]]]

What story does Genesis Chapters 1-11 tell?
The story of God and the whole world.	[[https://bibleproject.com/explore/video/genesis-1-11/|[ref 1]]]

What does God do in Genesis?
He creates order out of disorder.	[[https://bibleproject.com/explore/video/genesis-1-11/|[ref 1]]]

Who does God create in His image in Genesis?
Humans, or "Adam" in Hebrew.

What are humans made to be?	[[https://bibleproject.com/explore/video/genesis-1-11/|[ref 1]]]
* reflections of God's character
* representatives of God's rule
* harness creation's potential and create beauty and order


What represents humans' choice?
The Tree of Life 
OR
The Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil

What is the key choice that God presents to humans?
* trust God's definition of good and evil
* OR
* seize autonomy and define good and evil for themselves

What does the serpent represent?
A creature in rebellion against God.
