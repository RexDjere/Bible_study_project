What is the Holy Spirit?
For the majority of Christian denominations, the Holy Spirit, or Holy Ghost, 
is believed to be the third person of the Trinity, 
a Triune God manifested as God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit,
each entity itself being God.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holy_Spirit_in_Christianity|[ref 2]]]

What does the word Ruach mean?
Ruach is the Hebrew word for spirit, breath, or wind. [[https://firmisrael.org/learn/the-hebrew-word-ruach-and-gods-breath-in-our-lungs/|[ref 4]]]

What does Ruach Elohim mean?
Ruach Elohim means the Spirit of God. [[https://firmisrael.org/learn/the-hebrew-word-ruach-and-gods-breath-in-our-lungs/|[ref 4]]]

What does Ruach HaKodesh mean?
Ruach HaKodesh means the Holy Spirit. [[https://firmisrael.org/learn/the-hebrew-word-ruach-and-gods-breath-in-our-lungs/|[ref 4]]]

What does "Spirit of God" signify?
"Spirit of God" is used in the [[Home:01 Old Testament|Old Testament]] to signify "God's breath (Jacob 33.4).
