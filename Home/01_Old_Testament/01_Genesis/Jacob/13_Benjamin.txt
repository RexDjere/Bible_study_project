Who was Benjamin?
Benjamin was the last-born of Jacob's thirteen children (12 sons and one daughter), and the second and last son of Rachel in Jewish, Christian, and Islamic tradition.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin|[ref 1]]]
Benjamin was the youngest born son of Jacob, and he was the only son born in Canaan.   [[https://www.biblegateway.com/resources/all-men-bible/Benjamin|[ref 3]]]
