====== Israelite Tribe of Dan ======
Created Friday 01 October 2021

What is the Tribe of Dan?
The Tribe of Dan (meaning "Judge" in Hebrew) was one of the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Isaac:12 tribes of Israel|tribes of Israel]], according to the [[Home:01 Old Testament:Torah|Torah]] .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Dan|[ref 1]]]

What land was the Tribe of Dan allocated?
They were allocated a coastal portion of land where the people of Israel entered the Promised Land, later moving northwards. 	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Dan|[ref 1]]]

How large was the Tribe of Dan?
In the Biblical census of the [[+Book_of_Numbers]] , the tribe of Dan is portrayed as the second largest Israelite tribe (after [[Isaac:12 tribes of Israel:Israelite Tribe of Simeon:Tribe of Judah|Judah)]].	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Dan|[ref 1]]]
