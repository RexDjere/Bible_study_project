What is the Tribe of Levi?
According to the Bible, the Tribe of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:03 Levi|Levi]] is one of the tribes of Israel, traditionally descended from Levi, son of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]].	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Levi|[ref 1]]]

What were the duties of the Tribe of Levi?
The Tribe of Levi served particular religious duties for the Israelites, and had political respnsibilities as well.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Levi|[ref 1]]]
