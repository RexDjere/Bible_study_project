====== Abraham ======
Created Friday 01 October 2021

Who was Abraham?
Abraham (originally Abram) is the common patriarch of the [[+Abrahamic_religions]] , including [[+Judaism]] , [[+Christianity]] , and [[+Islam]] .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abraham|[ref 1]]]

Who is Abraham in Judaism?
In Judaism, he was the founding father of the [[+covenant_of_the_pieces]] , the special relationship between the [[+Hebrews]] and [[+God]] .

Who is Abraham in Christianity?
In Christianity, he was the spiritual progenitor of all believers, Jewish or [[+Gentile]] (non-Jewish).

Who is Abraham in Islam?
In Islam, he is seen as a link in the [[+chain_of_prophets]] that begins with [[+Adam]] and culminates in [[+Muhammad]] .
