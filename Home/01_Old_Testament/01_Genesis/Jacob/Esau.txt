What other name is Esau called?

What did Esau sell his birthright for?
red lentil pottage	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esau|[ref 1]]]

How did Esau give his birthright to his younger brother Jacob?	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esau|[ref 1]]]
* Esau returned home from hunting famished.
* He begged Jacob for some of the red lentil pottage that he had cooked.
* Jacob offered Esau a bowl of the red lentil stew in exchange for Esau's birthright.
* Esau agreed.
