Who was Reuben in relation to [[Jacob]] and [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Leah|Leah]] ? 
Reuben (also known as Re'uven) was the eldest son of Jacob and Leah.

What did Reuben found?
Reuben was the founder of the [[Isaac:12 tribes of Israel:Israelite Tribe of Reuben]] .

Which son of Jacob was Reuben?
Reuben was the firstborn son of Jacob by Leah. [[https://www.gotquestions.org/Reuben-in-the-Bible.html|[ref 2]]]

What is Reuben remembered for?
Reuben is remembered for being the forefather of one of the twelve tribes of Israel. [[https://www.gotquestions.org/Reuben-in-the-Bible.html|[ref 2]]]

Where is the story of Reuben told?
The story of Reuben is told in the Bible, the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis|Book of Genesis]] , chapters 29-50.

What happened to Reuben?
He had sex with his father's concubine [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Bilhah|Bilhah]].
His father, Jacob, was angry with Reuben, so he gave his birthright as Jacob's oldest son to his younger son [[12 Joseph|Joseph]].

What is the tomb of Reuben?	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nabi_Rubin|[ref 3]]]

* Nabi Rubin is named after a shrine in the village, believed by [[+Muslims]] to be the tomb of Reuben.

What happened to Al-Nabi Rubin?
It was captured by the ISrael Defense Forces during the 1948 Arab-Israeli War, and the inhabitants were expelled.
