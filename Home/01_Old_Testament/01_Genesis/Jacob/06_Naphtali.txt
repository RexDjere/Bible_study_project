who was Naphtali?
According to the Book of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis|Genesis,]] Naphtali (Hebrew for "my struggle") was the sixth son of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]], and second son with [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Bilhah|Bilhah]].

What tribe did Naphtali found?
HE was the founder of the [[Isaac:12 tribes of Israel:Israelite Tribe of Naphtali]] .
