Who was Asher?
Asher, in the Book of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis|Genesis]], is the second son of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]] and [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Zilpah|Zilpah]], 
and the founder of the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Isaac:12 tribes of Israel:Israelite Tribe of Asher|Tribe of Asher]].	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asher|[ref 1]]]

What does Asher mean?
The text of the Torah states that the name Asher means "happy" or "blessing".	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asher|[ref 1]]]

Where did Asher settle?
Asher and his four sons and daugther settled in [[+Canaan]] .	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asher|[ref 1]]]
