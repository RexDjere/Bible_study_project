Who was Gad?
Gad was, according to the Gook of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis|Genesis]], the first son of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]] and [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Zilpah|Zilpah]], the seventh son of Jacob overall, and the founder of the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Isaac:12 tribes of Israel:Israelite Tribe of Gad|Israelite tribe of Gad]].	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gad_(son_of_Jacob)|[ref 1]]]

What happened to the Tribe of Gad?
Following the Assyrian conquest of 721 BC, the 10 tribes were partially dispersed and eventually assimilated by other peoples.
The tribe of Gad thus became one of the [[Ten Lost Tribes of Israel]].	[[https://www.britannica.com/topic/Gad-Hebrew-tribe|[ref 3]]]
