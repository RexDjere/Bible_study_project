Who was Issachar?
Issachar was, according to the Book of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis|Genesis]], a son of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]] and [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Leah|Leah]] (the fifth son of Leah, and ninth son of Jacob), and the founder of the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Isaac:12 tribes of Israel:Israelite Tribe of Issachar|Israelite Tribe of Issachar]].
