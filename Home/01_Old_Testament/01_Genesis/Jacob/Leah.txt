Who was Leah in the Bible?
Leah is an important figure in the [[+Judeo_Christian_tradition]] , the unloved wife of the Biblical patriarch [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]].

Where does Leah first appear in the Bible?
Leah first appears in the Bible in the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis|Book of Genesis]], Chapter 29.

Name some of Leah's close relatives.
Leah is:
* the daughter of [[+Laban]]
* the older sister of [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Rachel|Rachel]]
* the niece of [[+Rebecca]]
* the grandaughter of [[+Bethuel]]
