Who was Bilhah?
Bilhah is a  woman mentioned in the Bible in the [[Book of Genesis]] . [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bilhah|[ref 1]]]

Where is Bilhah described in the Bible?
Genesis 29:29

How does Genesis 29:29 describe Bilhah?
 Genesis 29:29 describes her as [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Leah:Laban|Laban's]] handmaid, who was given to [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:Rachel|Rachel]] to be her handmaid on Rachel's marriage to [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob|Jacob]]. [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bilhah|[ref 1]]] 

How did Bilhah become Jacob's concubine?
When Rachel failed to have children, Rachel gave Bilhah to Jacob as a concubine to bear him children. [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bilhah|[ref 1]]] 

How many children did Bilhah give birth to for Jacob?
Bilhah gave birth to two sons, [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:05 Dan|Dan]] and [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:06 Naphtali|Naphtali]] .

What did Rachal di with Dan and Naphtali?
She claimed both boys as her own. [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bilhah|[ref 1]]]
