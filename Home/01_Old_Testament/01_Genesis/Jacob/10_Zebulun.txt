Who was Zebulun?
Zebulun was, according to the Books of Genesis and Numbers, the sixth and last son of Jacob and Leah, and the founder of the Israeliter Tribe of Zebulun.	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zebulun|[ref 1]]]
