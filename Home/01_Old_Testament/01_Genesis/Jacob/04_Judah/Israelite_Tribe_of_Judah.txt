What is the Tribe of Judah?
According to the [[+Hebrew_Bible]] , the tribe of Judah was one of the [[+Twelve_Tribes_of_Israel]] . 	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Judah|[ref 1]]] 

Break down the Tribe of Judah:	[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribe_of_Judah|[ref 1]]] 
* had conquests
* central capital in Jerusalem
* worshipped God [[+Yahweh]]
* figured prominently in [[+Deuteronomistic_history]]
* books of +Deuteronomy through [[+II_Kings]]
* during reign of Judahist reformer [[+Josiah]] from 641-609 DCE.
