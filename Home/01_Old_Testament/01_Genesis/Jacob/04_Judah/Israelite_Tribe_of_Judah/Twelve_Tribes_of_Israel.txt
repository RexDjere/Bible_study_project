Where are the 12 tribes of Israel listed?
In Deuteronomy 27:12-13

List the 12 tribes of Israel.
1. Reuben
2. Simeon
3. Levi
4. Judah
5. Issachar
6. Zebulun
7. Dan
8. Naphtali
9. Gad
10. Asher
11. Benjamin
12. Joseph

Which tribe later split into 2 half-tribes?
Joseph split into Ephraim and Manasseh.
