What is the Book of Kings?
The Book of Kings is a book of the [[Home:01 Old Testament:01 Genesis:Jacob:04 Judah:Israelite Tribe of Judah:Hebrew Bible|Hebrew Bible]]  and two books [[+1_Kings]] and [[+2_Kings]] in the Christian Old Testament .
It concludes the Deuteronomistic history , a history of Israel also including the books of [[+Joshua]] and [[+Judges]] and the Books of [[+Samuel]] .
