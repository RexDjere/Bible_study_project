What is the Old Testament?
The Old Testament is the first division of the [[+Christian_biblical_canon]] , which is based primarily upon the 24 books of the [[+Hebrew_Bible]] or Tanakh, a collection of ancient religious Hebrew writings by the [[+Israelites]] .
