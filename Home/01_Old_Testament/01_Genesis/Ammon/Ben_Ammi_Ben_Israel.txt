====== Ben Ammi Ben Israel ======

Who was Ben Ammi Ben-Israel?
Ben Ammi Ben-Israel (October 12, 1939 - December 27, 2014) was the American-born founder and spiritual leader of the African Hebrew Israelites of Jerusalem.

Where was Ben Ammi Ben-Israel born?
Ben Carter (later Ben Ammi Ben-Israel) was born in Chicago, Illinois, to a Baptist family.

What idea was Ben Ammi Ben Israel introduced to in 1961?
In 1961, a co-worker introduced him to the idea that African Americans are descendants of the Biblical Israelites.
