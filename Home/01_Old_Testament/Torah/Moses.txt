====== Moses ======
Created Wednesday 25 August 2021

Source doucments:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moses

Moses is considered to be the most important prophet in Judaism, and a very important prophet in Christianity, Islam, and other religions.
In Hebrew, Moses is known as Moshe Rabbenu which translates to "Moshe our Teacher".
Rabbinical Judaism has calculated Moses' lifespan to be from 1391 to 1271 BCE.
