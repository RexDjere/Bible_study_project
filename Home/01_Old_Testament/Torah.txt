====== Torah ======
Created Wednesday 25 August 2021

The Torah is the Hebrew name for the first 5 books of the Bible.
The Torah is also known as the Written Torah, and as the [[Pentateuch]] .
The 5 Books of the Torah were written by [[+Moses]] .
