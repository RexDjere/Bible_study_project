What is the Levant?
The Levant is an approximate historical geographical term referring to a large area in the Eastern Mediterranean region of Western Asia.

What is the Levant equivalent to in its narrowest sense?
In its narrowest sense, it is equivalent to the historical region of Syriam which included present day Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, Israel, Palestine, and most of TUrkey south-east of the middle Euphrates.
